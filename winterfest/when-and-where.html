When & Where

The Winterfest for this year will be held at North Coldopolis, IC. This year's winter festivities will begin on the 18th of December and they will continue until the 22nd of the same month. All of the activities will begin at 10:00 a.m. each day and they will conclude at 7:00 p.m. each night. The ice skating rinks can be found at 1534 Frigid Ln. Parking for the skiing slopes is located at 4952 Blizzard St. The address for the lodge where all indoor activities will be held is 2562 Frosty Ave. For all other activities, please follow the signs which will be placed to guide visitors to their locations. Maps will be provided at the lodge. Admissions for all visitors will be free. Notice: While we will do our best to keep the roads clear of ice and snow, we ask all visitors to please use caution and drive safely.

Winterfest 2023 - Nathan Roskelley
